# Legacy Operator

AI-powered marketing automation platform that transforms a simple business intake into a complete marketing system.

## Live Demo

[https://legacy-ai-marketing-suite.onrender.com]

## Overview

Legacy Operator helps service-based businesses generate marketing assets in minutes.

Users provide a simple business intake and receive:

* Social Media Posts
* Email Campaigns
* Ad Copy
* Lead Magnets
* Follow-Up Sequences
* Marketing Campaign Ideas

Built for:

* Insurance Professionals
* Real Estate Agents
* Consultants
* Coaches
* Agencies
* Local Businesses

## Features

### Landing Page

Premium SaaS-style marketing website featuring:

* Responsive design
* Modern branding
* Pricing section
* Marketing-focused copy
* Call-to-action funnels

### Intake Wizard

Multi-step business intake process designed to collect:

* Business information
* Target audience
* Marketing goals
* Service offerings

### Campaign Generation

Transforms business inputs into marketing assets and campaign recommendations.

### Deployment

* React
* Vite
* Tailwind CSS
* GitHub
* Render
